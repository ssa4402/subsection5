Freshmen Leaders on Campus

Freshmen Leaders on Campus (FLOC) emerged as a pivotal force in my life, especially during my initial transition to the United States. As a newcomer to a foreign land, I arrived in a state of uncertainty, knowing no one in this unfamiliar terrain. It was during this moment of vulnerability that FLOC graciously welcomed me into its fold.

Within the nurturing embrace of FLOC, I didn't merely find a group of acquaintances; I discovered a warm and inclusive community that went above and beyond in facilitating my assimilation into American college life. These individuals swiftly transformed into not just friends, but also mentors who provided the unwavering support and guidance that I direly needed to chart my path towards personal and academic success.
